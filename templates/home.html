About NeuroCare AI

Redefining Neuro-Diagnostics with Intelligence

NeuroCare AI is a hospital-grade brain tumor detection platform that bridges the gap between raw MRI data and actionable clinical insight — for doctors, patients, and administrators alike.

Explainable AI (XAI)

Grad-CAM heatmaps highlight regions of concern directly on the MRI so doctors can trust and verify the model's decision.

Secure & Compliant

Patient data is encrypted end-to-end with role-based access for Admin, Doctor, and Patient portals.

Multi-Role Portal

Dedicated dashboards for Admins to assign doctors, Doctors to predict & report, and Patients to view results.

What We Offer

Our Core Services

From intelligent MRI analysis to multilingual patient support, NeuroCare AI covers every step of the diagnostic journey.

AI-Powered MRI Analysis

Upload MRI scans and receive instant AI predictions with tumor classification, confidence scores, and visual heatmaps highlighting regions of interest.

Doctor Allocation System

Administrators can view real-time doctor schedules, departments, and duty timings, then assign the most suitable specialist to each incoming patient.

Doctor–Patient Messaging

Doctors can send personalised report summaries and messages directly to patients through the portal after reviewing AI predictions.

Multilingual Patient Portal

Patients can switch the interface language via a globe icon, with step-by-step guided registration and login instructions in their preferred language.

Patient Report Viewer

Patients get a clean, easy-to-understand view of their MRI results, doctor's notes, diagnosis, and any follow-up recommendations — all in one place.

Secure Data Management

All MRI scans, patient records, and reports are stored with encrypted access and visible only to authorised roles within the system.
